🎯 How to Play

🎮 Gameplay

  • • Word pairs fall from space with numbers
  • • English (left) and Swahili (right) columns
  • • Press two number keys directly to match pairs
  • • Example: Press 3 then 7 to match word 3 with word 7
  • • Don't let words reach Earth!

⚡ Power-ups

  • • 🛡️ Shield (Ctrl+1): Protect from one wave
  • • ⏰ Slow Time (Ctrl+2): Half speed temporarily
  • • 💎 Double Points (Ctrl+3): 2X score boost
